Galaxy Stadium

Stadium Information

Galaxy Stadium is the home of Texas Tech Football and one of the best game day environments in the Big 12. Located in the heart of campus, the stadium seats more than 60,000 fans and is surrounded by Raider Alley, tailgating, and plenty of Red Raider tradition.

Game Day Parking

Parking can be one of the most stressful parts of game day, but planning ahead makes a huge difference. Here are the options I recommend if you're visiting Lubbock for a Texas Tech football game.

My Favorite Option

My favorite way to park is by using Prked. You can rent a driveway from someone who lives near the stadium, usually for much less than official parking. It's an easy walk and makes leaving after the game much easier.

Official Parking

If you'd rather park on campus, the USA Auto Lot and S-1 Lot both offer single-game parking passes. These lots are close to the stadium but often sell out before game day.

Free Parking

Free parking is available near the United Supermarkets Arena and the Health Sciences Center. From there you can ride the Citibus shuttle to Raider Alley for a small fee.

Another Good Option

Parking near First Baptist Church on Broadway is another great choice. It's a little farther away, but the walk through campus is part of the game day experience.

Where to Stay

Overton Hotel & Conference Center

The closest luxury hotel to Galaxy Stadium and an easy walk to game day festivities.

Hyatt Place Lubbock

Modern rooms, free breakfast, and one of the best locations near campus.

Cotton Court Hotel

A boutique hotel with a unique West Texas feel just minutes from Texas Tech.

Hampton Inn & Suites University

A great family-friendly choice with free breakfast and convenient parking.

Staybridge Suites Lubbock

Affordable suites with kitchenettes, ideal for longer game weekend stays.

Where to Eat

  • Spanky's – A Texas Tech tradition famous for burgers and fried cheese.
  • Chimy's – Great tacos, drinks, and one of the most popular spots before kickoff.
  • One Guy Pizza – A favorite among students for quick pizza near campus.
  • Triple J Chophouse – Perfect if you're looking for a nicer dinner after the game.
  • Rosa's Café – Fast, affordable Tex-Mex with generous portions.

Game Day Tips

  • Arrive two to three hours before kickoff.
  • Check the weather before heading to the stadium.
  • Review the stadium bag policy before game day.
  • Wear your Texas Tech colors and be loud.
  • Take time to walk Raider Alley before entering the stadium.
  • Don't park anywhere that isn't marked for game day parking—you may get towed.
